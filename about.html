Our Story

Born from a Parent's
Need for Peace of Mind

Bundl began the night our co-founder sat beside a bassinet, checking her newborn's breathing every few minutes until sunrise. We knew there had to be a better way.

Why We Built Bundl

The first days and weeks with a newborn are magical — and terrifying. Every parent knows the feeling: you've finally gotten the baby to sleep, but instead of resting yourself, you're leaning over the crib every fifteen minutes just to make sure everything is okay.

We are a team of parents, biomedical engineers, and child health advocates who refused to accept that this anxiety was simply the price of parenthood. We believed technology could be gentle, wearable, and genuinely useful — not just another blinking gadget that creates more noise.

Bundl was built with one clear goal: give parents real information, in real time, without disrupting a single moment of precious baby sleep. A soft ankle band, a thoughtful app, and five vital signs tracked continuously — all designed with the kind of care you'd expect from someone who's been in that same exhausted, worried place themselves.

🌙

"I just wanted to know she was breathing. I didn't think that was too much to ask."

— Roopali, Co-founder & CEO

Our Mission & Values

Everything we build, every decision we make, begins with a single question: does this help parents feel more informed and less afraid?

🔬

Science First

Every feature, every threshold, every alert is grounded in pediatric research and validated clinical data. We never make health claims we can't support.

❤️

Parent-Centered Design

We design for exhausted parents at 3 AM, not for tech reviewers. Clarity over complexity. Calm over alarm. Information that empowers, not overwhelms.

🔒

Privacy by Default

Your baby's health data belongs to you. We use AES-256 encryption, collect only what's necessary, and never sell data to third parties — ever.

Sustainably Made
