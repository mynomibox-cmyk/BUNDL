Getting Started with Bundl

From box to monitoring in under 10 minutes.

1

Charge Your Band

Connect the magnetic cable. Full charge takes ~90 minutes.

💡 Amber = charging · Green = full
2

Download the App

Search "Bundl Baby" on App Store or Google Play. Free. iOS 14+ / Android 9+.

💡 Data is encrypted from first use
3

Pair via Bluetooth

Open app → tap "Add Device" → hold band near phone → tap to pair.

💡 Stay within 1m during first pairing
4

Set Up Baby's Profile

Enter date of birth, weight, and gestational age. Calibrates alert thresholds automatically.

💡 Editable anytime in app settings
5

Fit the Band

Sensor on inner ankle. Velcro snug — one finger should slide underneath.

💡 Never tight. Check skin daily.
6

Configure Alerts

Review thresholds and enable push notifications. Consult your pediatrician before adjusting defaults.

💡 Defaults follow pediatric guidelines

Troubleshooting

Most issues can be resolved in under 2 minutes. Try these steps first.

📡

Band Won't Connect to App

  1. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone
  2. Close and reopen the Bundl app
  3. Move phone within 1 metre of the band
  4. Press and hold the band's button for 3 seconds to restart it
  5. In app settings, tap "Forget Device" then re-pair
  6. If still failing, restart your phone and try again
💓

Heart Rate Reading Seems Wrong

  1. Check band fit — sensor must be on inner ankle, snug not tight
  2. Ensure baby is calm and relatively still during reading
  3. Clean the sensor window with a soft dry cloth
  4. Outlier readings during movement are normal — check trend averages
  5. Persistent unusual readings: consult your pediatrician
🔋

Battery Draining Too Fast

  1. Check that the band is not too far from phone (range exceeding 10m reduces efficiency)
  2. Reduce app sync frequency in Settings → Band → Sync Rate
  3. Ensure no other Bluetooth devices are competing for connection
  4. If battery life is consistently under 18 hours, contact support for a replacement check
🔔

Not Receiving Alerts

  1. Check phone notifications are enabled for the Bundl app
  2. Ensure Do Not Disturb does not block Bundl (add to "Allow" list)
  3. Verify alert thresholds are configured in app Settings → Alerts
  4. Check band is connected — alerts only fire when connected
  5. On iOS: check that Background App Refresh is enabled for Bundl
🌡️

Temperature Reading Looks High

  1. Skin temperature is typically 0.5–1°C lower than core body temperature
  2. Check room temperature — warm rooms raise skin readings
  3. Ensure band isn't covered by blanket (insulation causes elevation)
  4. Check baby for signs of overheating: sweaty neck, flushed cheeks
  5. If core temperature concern: use an accurate rectal or axillary thermometer
📱

App Crashing or Freezing

  1. Force-close the app and reopen it
  2. Check for and install app updates from App Store / Play Store
  3. Restart your phone
  4. If crashing persists: uninstall, reinstall — your data is saved to cloud
  5. Ensure your OS is updated to at least iOS 14 or Android 9

Getting the Most from the App
