SIDS & Safe Sleep

Knowledge Is the First Step to Prevention

Around 3,500 infants die from sleep-related causes in the US each year. Understanding the risks — and knowing how to monitor — gives you the confidence to act fast when it matters.

Safe Sleep Checklist

  • ✓ Always place baby on their back to sleep
  • ✓ Use a firm, flat sleep surface only
  • ✓ Keep sleep area clear of soft items
  • ✓ Maintain room temp 68–72°F
  • ✓ Share a room — not a bed
  • ✓ Use Bundl for continuous vital monitoring
