Bundl is designed for babies from newborn (birth) through 24 months. We offer three adjustable sizes to accommodate growing ankle circumferences from approximately 9cm (newborn) to 15.2cm (24 months). The velcro adjustability provides a precise fit within each size range.
Bundl is a wellness monitoring device, not a certified medical device. It is designed to give parents real-time data insights and should not be used as a replacement for medical assessment, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your pediatrician for any health concerns about your baby.
The built-in Lithium-polymer battery provides 24–36 hours of continuous monitoring. In standby mode, it can last up to 72 hours. Charging is quick and easy via the included magnetic pogo-pin connector. The app will alert you when the battery is running low.

The triple-layer comfort system (TENCEL + Bamboo + Organic Cotton) is specifically engineered for newborn skin sensitivity, with even pressure distribution to prevent irritation.
